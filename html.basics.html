About Us

11 months ago, founder Cardi O. Vascular stopped by Walgreens on the way home from Blockbuster and noticed the wealth of heart-shaped items in the holiday aisle – cards, boxes of chocolates, even Beanie Babies with heart-shaped embroidery. What else, she wondered, could be made into the shape of a heart? And with that, HSS was born. Months later, we’ve filled over 4 orders and counting!

What We Do

Here at HSS, we believe love can move mountains, and when you send us an item, any item, we will return it to you in the shape of a heart.
